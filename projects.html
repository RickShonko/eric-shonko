About Us

ECITIZEN-PHARMACY AND POISONS Education is an educational initiative designed to simplify pharmacy regulations and licensing procedures in Kenya. Our goal is to provide accurate, practical, and easy-to-understand educational content for pharmacy professionals, students, and healthcare organizations.

What We Will Offer

Educational Resources

  • Professional learning materials
  • Regulatory guides
  • Study resources
  • Knowledge articles

PPB Guidance

  • Registration process
  • License renewal
  • Compliance education
  • Regulatory updates

Training

  • Online courses
  • Professional webinars
  • Certification programs
  • Digital learning materials

Our Mission

To make pharmacy education and regulatory information more accessible, helping pharmacy professionals remain informed, compliant, and prepared through high-quality educational content and digital learning experiences.
